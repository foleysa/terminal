Section 232 — Universal 25% Steel & Aluminum Tariff
Unlike Section 301 (China-specific), Section 232 applies to ALL countries. There are no tariff-free origins for steel and aluminum. USMCA-qualifying goods from Canada/Mexico were exempt, but recent policy changes have tightened enforcement. Verify USMCA qualification with your customs broker on every shipment.
Copper Advantage
Copper is NOT subject to Section 232. Chilean and Peruvian copper enters at 0-3% MFN duty. If you can substitute copper for aluminum in any application, you eliminate the 25% tariff. Run the total cost comparison — copper's premium is narrowing fast.
Source: USTR, Section 232 Proclamations, CBP ACE Portal, ITC Antidumping/CVD Orders
