Key Tariff Risk
TiO₂ pigment from China faces 30.5% combined duty (25% Section 301 + 5.5% MFN). Chinese-origin polyester coatings face 28.7%. Shifting TiO₂ sourcing to Chemours (US/Mexico) or Tronox (Australia) eliminates the 25% surcharge entirely.
USMCA Opportunity
Mexico-origin coatings under HS 3210 enter at 0% duty under USMCA. PPG (Comex plants) and Sherwin-Williams both have Mexico manufacturing. If you're not requesting plant-of-origin options on RFQs, you're leaving tariff savings on the table.
